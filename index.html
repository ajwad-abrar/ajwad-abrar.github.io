Research Profile

About My Work

I am a Junior Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Islamic University of Technology and an MSc student in CSE at the same institution. I am building a research profile for PhD applications focused on trustworthy language technologies, low-resource NLP, and practical evaluation methods for modern AI systems.

My recent work spans cultural alignment in LLMs, Bangla consumer health query summarization, educational AI, cyberbullying detection, efficient low-resource NLP, and bias analysis in language and multimodal models. I am especially interested in research that combines methodological rigor with social relevance in multilingual and underrepresented contexts.
